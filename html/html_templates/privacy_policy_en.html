Data Protection

VENDO will process the personal data provided by the Member in connection with the execution of the contract in compliance with the relevant legal provisions. If the Member resides within the EU or the contract is performed within the EU, the provisions of the EU GDPR which is referred to below shall specifically apply.

VENDO collects and processes member data in order to provide its services. During registration as a Member, the following data in particular are collected and processed by VENDO:

  • Referral code
  • Selected language
  • Name of the member
  • Date of birth
  • Address
  • Contact details (email, telephone)
  • Bank account / Payment method
  • Photo

VENDO collects and processes the following additional data when using VENDO services, in particular the back office account:

  • Number of members in the company downline
  • Number of direct members
  • Volumes achieved
  • Information on status and volumes generated
  • Member-initiated trade transactions and history
  • Commissions
  • Statistical and performance data on the trade transactions carried out
  • Information on automation operations

It is inherent in the nature of a network of members that member data is made available to other members in a certain form. This includes data visible in the back office such as

  • Name of the member
  • Photo
  • Number of direct members and in the downline
  • Downline volume
  • Ranking list data

The personal data required for the execution of the contract are processed by VENDO for the purposes of order processing (contacting, service provision, invoicing) (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR) and are required for this purpose. VENDO shall treat these as confidential. Data shall not be passed on to third parties, unless this is necessary for the execution of the order (e.g. service providers, such as the Trading Platforms, necessary for the order) or due to a legitimate interest (e.g. tax consultants, collection service providers, other members in the context of the provision of services inherent in the member network) or required by law (e.g. due to inquiries from tax and investigating authorities). If the data are no longer required for order processing and if there are no warranty periods and/or legal storage obligations to the contrary, the data shall be deleted.

Data subjects have the right to free information concerning the data stored in relation to their persons. The data subject shall also have the right to rectification, to deletion, to restrict processing, to object to processing and to data portability, if applicable.

If personal data are processed on the basis of the Member's consent, the Member can revoke this consent at any time with future effect.

If personal data are collected on the basis of legitimate interests according to Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR, the Member has the right, in accordance with Art. 21 GDPR, to file an objection to processing with future effect if there are reasons for doing so arising from his particular situation or if the objection is aimed against direct marketing. In the latter case, the Member has a general right to object which is implemented without specifying a particular situation.

The Member can contact a data protection supervisory authority in the case of complaints.

Processing of personal data when visiting the website

When using the website for informational purposes only, VENDO collects the personal data that the browser sends to the VENDO server. If the Member wishes to view the VENDO website, VENDO collects the following data on the basis of legitimate interests to the extent the data is technically necessary to display the website to the Member and to ensure the stability and security (legal basis within the EU is Art. 6 para. 1 sentence 1 lit. f GDPR):

IP address, date and time of the request, time zone difference to Greenwich Mean Time (GMT), content of the request (specific page), access status/HTTP status code, amount of data transferred in each case, website from which the request originates, browser, operating system and its interface, language and version of the browser software.

The data are stored in log files. The data are generally deleted after one month at the latest.

Use of the email address for advertising purposes

VENDO reserves the right to contact Members by email through the email address they provided in order to advise them of similar services and offers and to notify the Members of new offers. The Member can object to the use of his email address for this advertising purpose at any time without incurring any costs other than the transmission costs in accordance with the basic rates.
